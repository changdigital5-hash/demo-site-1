About Us

Discover the story behind Port Dover's beloved waterfront restaurant.

OUR STORY

From Humble Beginnings to Lakeside Legend

Nestled on the picturesque shores of Lake Erie, The Beach House began as a small seaside eatery in the heart of Port Dover, Ontario. What started as a simple fish and chips shack — serving golden-fried Lake Erie perch to locals and summer visitors — quickly became a beloved gathering place for the entire community.

Over the years, our passion for fresh, locally sourced ingredients and our commitment to warm, genuine hospitality transformed that modest shack into the waterfront dining destination it is today. The stunning lake views that frame every meal, the sound of waves lapping against the shore, and the unforgettable sunsets over the water — these are the things that keep our guests coming back, season after season.

Today, The Beach House stands as a testament to the power of great food, beautiful surroundings, and the kind of heartfelt service that makes every guest feel like family.

OUR KITCHEN

Fresh, Local, Unforgettable

At The Beach House, our culinary philosophy is simple: let the ingredients shine. We source the freshest Lake Erie perch and pickerel directly from local fishermen, ensuring every fillet that reaches your plate is as fresh as the lake breeze. Our partnerships with Ontario farms mean that our seasonal produce is always at peak flavor and quality.

Our talented kitchen team takes pride in crafting menus that celebrate the bounty of the region. From classic lakeside favorites to innovative seasonal specials, every dish is prepared with meticulous attention to detail and an unwavering dedication to quality. We believe that great food doesn't need to be complicated — it just needs to be made with the best ingredients and a whole lot of heart.

Whether you're savoring our signature perch platter or indulging in a seasonal creation, you'll taste the difference that fresh, local sourcing makes in every single bite.
