Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about dining at The Beach House.

Where is The Beach House located?

▼

We're located at 2 Walker St, Port Dover, ON N0A 1N0, right on the waterfront of Lake Erie. Get Directions →

What are your hours of operation?

▼

Sunday through Thursday 11am to 8:30pm, Friday and Saturday 11am to 9pm. Hours may vary on holidays.

Do you accept reservations?

▼

Yes! We recommend reservations especially on weekends and holidays. Call us at (519) 583-0880 or use our contact form.

Is there parking available?

▼

Yes, there is free street parking available on Walker St and nearby lots. During peak summer months, we recommend arriving early.

Do you have a patio?

▼

Yes! We have a beautiful waterfront patio overlooking Lake Erie. It's open seasonally, weather permitting.

Is the restaurant wheelchair accessible?

▼

Yes, our restaurant is fully wheelchair accessible including the main dining room, washrooms, and patio.

Do you have a dress code?

▼

We have a casual, relaxed atmosphere. Come as you are — beach attire welcome!

Do you accommodate large groups?

▼

Absolutely! We can accommodate groups of up to 40 guests. Please call ahead for parties of 8 or more. A 15% gratuity is added for large parties.

Do you offer vegetarian or vegan options?

▼

Yes, we have several vegetarian options and can accommodate vegan dietary needs. Please ask your server for details.

Can you accommodate food allergies?

▼

Absolutely. Please inform your server of any allergies or dietary restrictions and our kitchen will do their best to accommodate you.

What is your price range?

▼

Most entrees are in the $20–30 range per person. We also have appetizers starting at $8 and a kids menu available.

Do you have a kids menu?

▼

Yes! Our kids menu features child-friendly favorites like chicken fingers, grilled cheese, and fish & chips, all for $10 or less.

Do you host private events?

▼

Yes, we offer private dining and event hosting for birthdays, anniversaries, corporate events, and more. Contact us for details.

Do you offer catering?

▼

We offer full-service catering for events of all sizes. Call us or send a message through our contact form to discuss your needs.

Still Have Questions?

Our team is happy to help with any questions not covered here.
