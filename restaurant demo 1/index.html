About Us

A Lakeside Dining Experience Like No Other

Nestled along the sandy shores of Lake Erie, The Beach House has been Port Dover's premier waterfront dining destination since its establishment. Our family-owned restaurant combines the charm of beachside dining with a commitment to fresh, locally-sourced seafood and seasonal ingredients.

Whether you're enjoying a casual lunch with panoramic lake views or a romantic sunset dinner, every visit to The Beach House is crafted to be an unforgettable experience. We invite you to taste the difference that passion and fresh ingredients make.
